Privacy Policy

Effective Date: July 1, 2025

1. Introduction

At TravelNest, we are committed to protecting your personal data and respecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your information.

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To personalize your travel experience
  • To manage bookings and respond to inquiries
  • To improve our website and services
  • To send promotional offers (if opted in)

4. Data Security

We take strong precautions to protect your data from unauthorized access, alteration, or disclosure.

5. Your Rights

You can request access to your data, ask for corrections, or request deletion by contacting us at privacy@travelnest.com.

6. Policy Updates

We may occasionally update this policy. Changes will be posted here, so please check back regularly.

If you have any questions, contact us at support@travelnest.com.
